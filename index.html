About Us

10+ Years Experience Delivering Sustainable Solar Energy Solutions

Leading Solar Energy Company in India

Founded with a vision to promote clean energy, Rudra Urja has successfully installed 1000+ solar projects across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

We provide complete solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction) solutions, including rooftop solar systems, solar water heaters, solar street lights, and large-scale solar power plants.

Our mission is to reduce carbon footprint while helping customers save on electricity bills with innovative and cost-effective solar technology.
